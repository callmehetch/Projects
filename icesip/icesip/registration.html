Conference Registration
At least one author of each accepted paper must register at full fee before 15 th April, 2019. Papers without at least one registered author cannot be presented and published. Each full fee registration is entitled to present and publish up to a maximum of one (1) manuscript. The manuscript may contain a maximum of six pages. Manuscripts in excess of six pages will not be considered for further processing.
